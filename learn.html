Introduction to IT

Information Technology (IT) is all about using computers, software, networks, and systems to manage and process information efficiently. It is a broad field with many specialties and applications.

Key Areas of IT

  • Software Development: Creating programs, applications, and websites.
  • Networking: Connecting computers and devices to share data.
  • Cybersecurity: Protecting systems and data from attacks.
  • Database Management: Storing and organizing large amounts of information.
  • Cloud Computing: Using remote servers to store and process data online.
  • IT Support: Helping people troubleshoot technology problems.
